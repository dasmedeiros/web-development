I help organizations make better decisions by translating raw information into tangible ideas. To achieve the best outcomes, I can employ a range of the most modern data-driven techniques and tools such as SQL, Python, Spark, Power BI, and Tableau.

In 2014, I started a bachelor's in Computer Science, but without a sense of belongingness, I decided to drop out. In 2016, I was accepted into one of the most prestigious universities in Brazil to pursue my Business degree. In the following 5 years, with a combination of in- and off-campus experiences in different businesses and organizational areas I developed a robust skillset ranging from critical thinking, fast learning, and easy adaptability in multicultural environments.

In 2021, after a successful and enriching internship in a global company and just before graduating I landed a job in the booming field of data science. I worked as a Business Intelligence Analyst solving problems for major financial institutes in Brazil. In a span of 15 months, I was able to lay the analytics foundations for 3 big players by helping them launch an AI in the form of chatbots which generated massive amounts of data. As the 'data guy' I was responsible for defining data tracking, data manipulation, and translating both structured and unstructured data it into actionable insights.

Now in 2022, I made an ambitious move to Canada to expand my knowledge in data science by taking a data analytics post-graduate program at Durham College. My willingness to change, working hard skills, and high motivation are making this the most life-changing experience.
